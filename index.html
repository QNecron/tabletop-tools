Tabletop Tools

Welcome to Tabletop Tools, below you'll find several tools that will hopefull help you as they've helped me during my gaming sessions. Currently there is Spells filter, only Core at this time, and a handy little Initiative tool that will take your PC's and NPC's rolls and order them for you automatically.

This toolset is made to be mobile friendly (phone & tablets) and I'm working on getting it to be as fast as possible. Please be patient if you happen to encounter a bug, I do this in my free time due to my tabletop gaming passion so I won't be able to address everything quickly.
